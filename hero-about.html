About The Tournament

What if I told you...

That true greatness isn't measured at Augusta or St. Andrews... but at a riverside casino golf course tucked away in the Midwest.


Welcome to the Rising Star Classic — 36 holes, two grueling competitions, one legendary day. A battle of stamina, questionable judgment, and the kind of raw talent that can only be fueled by gas station breakfast sandwiches and sheer willpower. Championships are not just won here — they are survived.
