About The Tournament

The Rising Star Classic is an annual golf tournament held at the prestigious Links at Rising Star Casino. This two-man scramble event brings together golf enthusiasts for a day of competition, camaraderie, and challenging play.

With 18 tee times available and a variety of prizes and payouts, the Rising Star Classic has become one of the region's most anticipated golf events.

Event Details

Date: April 2025

Location: The Links at Rising Star Casino

Format: 2-Man Scramble & 4-Man Scramble

Buy-in: $50.00 per person

Important Notes

Arrive at the course by 6:30 AM

Hawaiian themed attire encouraged

PACE OF PLAY is CRITICAL with 18 tee times

Please act appropriately around the clubhouse

Tournament Rules

  • $50.00 Mandatory Buy-in
  • 1 Mulligan per Person for HAWAIIAN themed clothes (2 man only)
  • Triple Bogey Max
  • No Gimmies
  • MUST SUBMIT SCORECARD TO JASON - Completely filled out or you are DQ'd
  • Can move ball 1 club length no closer to hole but CANNOT improve lie (rough, fairway, sand-rough)
  • $50.00 is due to Jason IN CASH BEFORE YOU TEE OFF
