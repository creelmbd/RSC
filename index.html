About The Tournament

What if I told you...

That true greatness isn't measured at Augusta or St. Andrews... but at a riverside casino golf course tucked away in the Midwest.


Welcome to the Rising Star Classic — 36 holes, two grueling competitions, one legendary day. A battle of stamina, questionable judgment, and the kind of raw talent that can only be fueled by gas station breakfast sandwiches and sheer willpower. Championships are not just won here — they are survived.

Event Details

Format: 2-Man Scramble & 4-Man Scramble

Important Notes

Arrive at the course by 6:30 AM

Hawaiian themed attire encouraged

PACE OF PLAY is CRITICAL with 18 tee times

Please act appropriately around the clubhouse

Tournament Rules

  • $50.00 Mandatory Buy-In — Due in cash to Jason before teeing off.
  • 1 Mulligan per Person — Awarded for wearing Hawaiian-themed attire (2-Man Scramble only).
  • Mulligans are NOT allowed on putts.
  • Triple Bogey Maximum — Pick up your ball after triple bogey.
  • No Gimmies — Every shot must be holed out.
  • Mandatory Scorecard Submission — Fully completed scorecards must be turned in to Jason. Incomplete scorecards will result in disqualification.
  • One Club Length Relief — You may move your ball up to one club length, no closer to the hole, but you cannot improve your lie (e.g., you must stay in rough if you're in rough, sand if you're in sand, etc.).
  • No Club Length Movement on Greens — Ball must be played as it lies on the putting surface.
  • Cards turned into Chris - Number of strokes only no squares, circles, triangles and especially hexagons
  • Tiebreakers — Ties will be broken by comparing scores on the hardest handicap hole first. If still tied, scores will be compared on the 2nd hardest, 3rd hardest, and so on until a winner is determined.
