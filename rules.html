Tournament Rules

🎟️ Entry & Buy-In

  • $50.00 Mandatory Buy-In — Due in cash to Jason before teeing off.

🏌️‍♂️ Mulligans & Attire

  • 1 Mulligan per Person — Awarded for wearing Hawaiian-themed attire (2-Man Scramble only).
  • Mulligans are NOT allowed on putts.

📝 Scorecard Rules

  • Mandatory Scorecard Submission — Fully completed scorecards must be turned in to Chris.
  • Number of strokes only — No squares, circles, triangles, or especially hexagons.
  • Incomplete scorecards will result in disqualification.

⛳ Play Rules

  • Triple Bogey Maximum — Pick up your ball after reaching triple bogey on a hole.
  • No Gimmies — Every shot must be holed out (no "that's good").

🏌️‍♂️ Ball Movement / Relief

  • One Club Length Relief — You may move your ball up to one club length, no closer to the hole.
    • Cannot improve your lie (e.g., must stay in rough if you're in rough, sand if you're in sand, etc.).
  • No Club Length Movement on Greens — Ball must be played as it lies on the putting surface.

🏆 Tiebreaker Rules

  • Ties will be broken by comparing scores on:
    • The hardest handicap hole first.
    • If still tied, the 2nd hardest hole, then 3rd hardest, and so on until a winner is determined.
